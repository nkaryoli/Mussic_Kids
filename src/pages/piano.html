With the Mouse

  1. Click to Play Notes
    • Click on any piano key to play it.
    • There will be an immediate response, and the assigned note will play.
    • You can click multiple keys in succession to play a melody.

Using Both Keyboard and Mouse

  1. Combine Inputs
    • You can combine both methods: play some notes using the keyboard and others with the mouse.
    • Press a key and click at the same time to create harmonics.
    • This allows for faster playing and more creative musical expression.
